Our Features

Everything You Need
to Get Hired

Eight AI-powered modules working together to give you an unfair advantage in the job market.

Core

AI Resume Analyzer

Upload your resume and get instant, in-depth analysis. Our AI scores your resume, identifies skill gaps, and suggests powerful improvements — with ATS optimization built in.

Smart Job Matching

Go beyond keywords. Our algorithm understands context and culture to match you with roles where you'll genuinely thrive.

Skill Verification

Dynamic questions verify the skills you claim. Earn verified badges that boost your profile credibility with employers.
