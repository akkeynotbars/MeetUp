How It Works

From resume to offer
in three steps

No complicated setup. Upload your CV and MeetUp handles the rest — AI-powered, end to end.

01

Upload Your CV

Drop your resume and our AI instantly analyzes your skills, experience, and profile. You'll get a score, breakdown, and improvement tips in seconds.

02

Get Smart Matches

Browse jobs with live compatibility scores. Our algorithm shows exactly how well your profile fits each role — so you only apply where you'll shine.

03

Apply & Get Hired

Apply in one click, practice with AI mock interviews, and manage all your applications — shortlisted, rejected, pending — from one clean dashboard.
